What Is Mental Health?

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects the way we think, feels, and act. This usually helps identify how someone deals with stress and problems. Most psychological factors are what mainly contribute to mental illness, for example, losing someone very close to you, abuse, trauma, etc.

What To Look For?

Everyone can experience different conditions considering there are many types of mental health issues. A few common ones are anxiety disorders, mood disorders, Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder, Eating disorders, etc. if you would like to know more about these disorders visit, "Mental health.gov."

Early signs (These are a few out of many more):

  • Eating or sleeping too much or too little
  • Pulling away from people and usual activities
  • low or no energy
  • Feeling numb or like nothing matters
