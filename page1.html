About Mental Health

What Is Mental Health?

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects the way we think, feel, and act. This usually helps identify how someone deals with stress and problems. Most psychological factors are what mainly contribute to mental illness, for example, losing someone that’s very important to you, abuse, trauma, etc.

What To Look For?

Everyone can experience different things; considering there are many types of mental health issues. A few very common ones are anxiety disorders, mood disorders, Obsessive-Compulsive Disorder, Eating disorders, etc. if you would like to know more about these disorders visit, "Mental health.gov."

Early signs (These are a few out of many more):

  • Eating or sleeping too much or too little
  • Pulling away from people and usual activities
  • low or no energy
  • Feeling numb or like nothing matters

If the situation is life-threatening please call the following number.
-Emergency Medical Services—911
